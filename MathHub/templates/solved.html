List of Solved Math Problems

  • Problem 1: Solve for x in the equation x^2 + 2x - 3 = 0. Answer: x = 1 or x = -3.
  • Problem 2: Find the area of a triangle with base 5 cm and height 10 cm. Answer: 25 cm^2.
  • Problem 3: What is the slope of the line that passes through the points (1, 2) and (3, 4)? Answer: 1.
